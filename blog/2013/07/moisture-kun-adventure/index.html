湿度君的一天

Hi, I’m father of moisture kun.

I got this idea from “super brothers sword and sworcery”, a beautiful ios game telling a story of sacrifice.

This prototype comes from Scythian, the main character in Sword & Sworcery EP. I made another story of her, hope you like it.

A long long time ago, moisture was born in a desert with out any rain, river. But a gift was given, he can mesture the moisture of a land or soil, and show it before him. To save his country, he went on the way finding Neverland “Dream Blue”, where there is enough water. Dogfella is his fav pet, guarding him trough forests, snow mountains and great valley…

So, Let’s see his story…

So what happened to moisture kun at last?
